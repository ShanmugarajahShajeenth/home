What is Chess?

Chess is a popular game that has been around since centuries. It's a two player game consisting an 8 by 8 checkered board with black and white squares. There are 8 pieces on both sides, which are differentiated by color, usually black or white. The point of the game to put the opponents king in checkmate, before they do to you. The game ends when the king cannot move anywhere, and is being threatened to be captured.
